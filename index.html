About Dr. Carpio

Dr. Maria Abigail Carpio, known as Aby, is a seasoned development economist with extensive experience in market systems development, particularly in agriculture, food systems, and the financial sector. She has worked across various regions, including Africa, Asia, and Latin America, focusing on rural and agricultural finance.
